## The Subscription Math Nobody Talks About

Let's be honest about what field service software actually costs:

- **Jobber** — $50 to $200/month depending on how many users you have
- **ServiceTitan** — $250 to $500/month, plus setup fees
- **Housecall Pro** — $50 to $200/month

That's anywhere from $600 to $6,000 a year. Every year. Forever. For software you don't own, can't customize, and can take away from you at any time.

Now add it up over five years. You've spent $3,000 to $30,000 on software that still doesn't belong to you.
